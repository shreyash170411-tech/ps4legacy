About the PS4

The PlayStation 4 is an eighth-generation home video game console released by Sony Interactive Entertainment on November 15, 2013. It succeeded the PlayStation 3 and competed with the Xbox One and Nintendo Switch.

Its blend of powerful hardware, an unmatched exclusive library, and a friendly developer platform made it a defining console of its era — and one of the best-selling consoles of all time.
